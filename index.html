THIS IS THE STORY OF "THE HUNGRY WOLF"


The story of the hungry wolf is about a wolf who is
very hungry band goes to a farm to try and find food.
The wolf sees a pig in a pen and tries to eat it,
but the pig is too big and the wolf can't get it.
The wolf sees a chicken in a coop and tries to eat it,
but the chicken is too fast and the wolf can't catch it.

“A liar is never believed even when he speaks the truth.”
This short story clearly states the importance of always speaking the truth.
The boy who cried wolf helps children understand that you cannot speak lies and get away with it.
